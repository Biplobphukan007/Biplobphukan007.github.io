OUR AIM

Youth Societal Club aims at providing relief to all the people who are vulnerable to any kind calamity,epidemic, pandemic or any sorts of upheaval.

Project Seva

YSCT was able to help around 60 underprivileged families hit with the covid crisis from different places of Jorhat with relief kits and 20 homeless people with lunch packets .

Project Flood Relief

YSCT was able to help around 200 families with flood relief from 3 diff flood affected districts of Assam during the last two years (2019-2020)
